====== tcpdump ======

=====tutorial=====
  http://openmaniak.com/tcpdump.php

=====list all traffic=====
  sudo tcpdump

=====list all interfaces=====
  sudo tcpdump -D

=====log traffic on interface eth0=====
  sudo tcpdump -i eth0
  
=====list traffic on certain port=====
  sudo tcpdump port 443
