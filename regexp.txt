====== Regular Expressions  ======

===== Online tools =====
* http://regexr.com/

===== expr =====
<code bash>
expr "ok T:83.4 /0.0 B:0.0 /0.0 @:0" : 'ok T:\([0-9]*\.[0-9]*\)'
</code>

=====sed & cut=====
<code bash>
grep -o '\(T:\)\([0-9]*\.[0-9]*\)' /tmp/UltiFi/ttyACM0/ temp.out | cut -c 3-
</code>
