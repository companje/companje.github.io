====== TAR ======

==tar without compression==
<code>tar cf backup.tar folder</code>

==to tar a folder (with all subfolders and files):==
<code>tar czf backup.tgz thefolder</code>

==to untar a file==
<code>tar xvzf file.tar.gz</code>

==untar to current folder (removing first component of path in archive)==
removes 'wordpress/' from path
<code>tar xvzf latest.tar.gz --strip-components=1</code>
