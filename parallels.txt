==network bridge==
bridge the nework through Virtual Machine->Configure->Network to get in the same subnet as the Host operating system.

==connect from OSX terminal to Ubuntu terminal==
* make sure `ssh` is installed on Ubuntu
* just use the VM's ip address to connect `ssh you@10.1.2.3`
